Operational Protocol
03 / 04

Four phases.
Zero compromises.

Every AXIOM restoration follows the same disciplined sequence — a protocol refined across thousands of projects, where each decision is documented, reversible, and executed with the precision the original deserves.

I

Intake & Triage

Physical and spectral assessment of the source medium. We determine degradation type, establish a baseline signal profile, and assign a restoration classification.

II

Capture

Custom playback system calibration and high-resolution transfer. Playback speed, bias, and EQ curves are tuned to the specific format and era of the source.

III

Restoration

Manual and algorithmic noise reduction, drop-out repair, wow and flutter correction, and spectral reconstruction — applied with a light hand guided by the original signal.

IV

Delivery

Archival masters, access copies, and full restoration documentation. You receive both the signal and its story.
